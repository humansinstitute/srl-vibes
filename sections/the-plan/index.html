The Plan

Four steps. Each one builds on the last. By the end you'll your personalised, mobile productivity app.

1. Small Change: Change the title and make it yours (5-10 mins)

2. Bigger Change: Can we replace Trello with a simple alternative? (10-20 mins)

3. Perosnalise: run wild with your ideas (30 mins)

4. Autonomous Agents: Sneak peak into automating work with Wingman agents (10-20 mins)
