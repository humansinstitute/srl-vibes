The Spectrum of AI Knowledge

Its magic we're all going to die

What actually is AI?

At the end of the day, it is a computer that can talk back, but it has read a lot more than us.

AI is just software that recognizes patterns really well and uses them to predict the next best thing to say or do.

How prompts work

Think of it like finishing a sentence, but at super‑speed.

  • The model reads your words and turns them into numbers it can understand.
  • It guesses the next word based on patterns it learned from lots of text.
  • It keeps guessing, word by word, until it builds a full response.

Why use AI?

AI bends the curve — it makes time and effort go further than they used to.

It is leverage: drafts faster, explores more options, and helps you ship sooner.

Set & Setting

Your mindset and environment matter. Treat AI like a collaborator, not a vending machine.

Be clear about goals, share context, and iterate with feedback. That is how you get great results.
